Ahsan Habib Muaz

Bangladeshi web developer, graphic designer, safety professional, and digital innovator based in Saudi Arabia, recognized for his diverse skill set in web development, ethical hacking, and industrial safety practices.

Early Life and Education

Born in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Muaz demonstrated an early passion for technology and creativity. He completed his secondary education at Tanshirul Islam Cadet Madrasa (SSC) with a GPA of 4.38, followed by higher secondary studies at Govt. Padma College (HSC) with a GPA of 4.50. He later pursued a Bachelor of Social Science (BSS) at Padma University, graduating with a CGPA of 3.84.

Career

Web Development and Graphic Design

Driven by his passion for digital creativity, Muaz has excelled as a web developer and graphic designer. He has crafted multiple websites and digital projects, focusing on performance optimization, user-centric design, and modern aesthetics. His portfolio website, ahsanhabibmuaz.github.io, showcases his innovative use of CSS, JavaScript, and graphic design tools like Adobe Illustrator, CapCut, and PicsArt.

Safety and Industrial Work

Muaz began his professional career as a document controller at DNK International Private Limited (February 2022 – August 2022) before transitioning into specialized safety roles. Now based in Saudi Arabia, he is known for his expertise as a safety professional, working in positions such as Standby Man and Firewatch. His high safety standards are reinforced by certifications from TÜV, Human Assets Training Institute (HATI), MEIRC Saudi Arabia, and Total Safety, along with specialized training in SCABA Donning & Doffing.
